Bloom lets you preview both your customer-facing store and your admin dashboard. Switch between Store and Admin modes to see different parts of your ecommerce store.

## What are Store and Admin Modes?

While building your store, you often need to see how your store looks to customers and manage your store's products, orders, and settings.

Bloom provides two preview modes so you can see different parts of your project: [Store mode](#store-mode) and [Admin mode](#admin-mode). Switch between these modes anytime to check your store's design and manage your store's backend while chatting with Bloom.

The mode you're viewing doesn't affect what you can ask Bloom to do. You can ask Bloom to make changes to your store or admin dashboard regardless of which mode you're viewing.

### Store Mode

Store mode shows your customer-facing store. This is what your customers see when they visit your online store.

Use Store mode to:

- Design your store's appearance and layout.
- Update your homepage, product pages, and collection pages.
- Check how your store looks to customers.
- Preview store changes before publishing.
- Review product displays and navigation.

![Store mode preview showing homepage](https://res.cloudinary.com/dza7lstvk/image/upload/v1770724050/Bloom/CleanShot_2026-02-10_at_13.47.20_2x_ghbxct.png)

### Admin Mode

Admin mode shows your admin dashboard. This is where you manage your store's products, orders, customers, and settings.

Use Admin mode to:

- View and manage products, collections, and inventory.
- Check orders and customer information.
- Configure store settings like shipping and payments.
- Manage custom features.

![Admin mode preview showing the orders list](https://res.cloudinary.com/dza7lstvk/image/upload/v1770724073/Bloom/CleanShot_2026-02-10_at_13.47.48_2x_ofv1tj.png)

---

## Switch Between Modes

To switch between Store and Admin modes in your Bloom project interface:

1. Click the Store tab at the top of the preview to view your customer-facing store.
2. Click the Admin tab at the top of the preview to view your admin dashboard.

Switch between modes anytime to check different parts of your project while chatting with Bloom.
